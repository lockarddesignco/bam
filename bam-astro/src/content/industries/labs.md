  - question: "How does AHU degradation affect cleanroom classification?"
    answer: "Fouled coils restrict airflow and reduce the air change rates that maintain cleanroom pressurization and particulate control. A lab operating at ISO 7 can drift to ISO 8 or worse if supply air volume drops below specification. BAM's AHU restoration recovers static pressure and airflow to design parameters, protecting your cleanroom classification."
  - question: "What documentation does BAM provide for GMP-regulated lab environments?"
    answer: "Every project produces documentation meeting Good Manufacturing Practice standards: calibrated instrument readings, filter certification reports, before-and-after photographic evidence, equipment condition assessments, and deviation logs.
